About The Enchanted Flame

Founded with a love for cozy evenings and mindful rituals, The Enchanted Flame creates small-batch, hand-poured candles designed to soothe and enchant.

Our Philosophy

We believe great candles begin with intentional ingredients. We use sustainably-sourced wax blends, phthalate-free fragrances, and cotton wicks. Packaging is recyclable, and we minimize waste.

How we make them

  1. We blend soy and coconut wax for a clean, long burn.
  2. Fragrance oils are chosen for depth and performance.
  3. Each jar is hand-poured, cured, and inspected.

Sustainability

We prioritize compostable packing, recyclable glass, and local suppliers where possible. For bulk or wholesale orders, we offer eco-conscious options.
